Accessibility

Our commitment to making Wiser usable and inclusive for everyone.

Our Commitment

At Wiser, we believe education should be accessible to everyone. We are committed to ensuring that our platform is usable by people of all abilities and disabilities. We continuously work to improve the accessibility of our platform in line with WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards.

Perceivable

Content is presented in ways that all users can perceive, with text alternatives, captions, and proper contrast.

Operable

All features are accessible via keyboard navigation. No time limits that can't be extended.

Understandable

Clear language, predictable navigation, and helpful error messages throughout.

Robust

Compatible with assistive technologies including screen readers, voice control, and magnification software.

Accessibility Features

  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements are accessible via keyboard (Tab, Enter, Escape, Arrow keys).
  • Screen Reader Support: Semantic HTML, ARIA labels, and roles ensure compatibility with popular screen readers.
  • Dark Mode: A high-contrast dark theme reduces eye strain and improves readability in low-light conditions.
  • Responsive Design: The platform adapts to all screen sizes and orientations.
  • Focus Indicators: Clear visual indicators show which element is currently focused.
  • Alt Text: Images include descriptive alternative text for screen readers.
  • Color Contrast: Text and interactive elements meet WCAG AA contrast ratio requirements.
  • Resizable Text: Content remains functional when text is resized up to 200%.

Video Content

We encourage instructors to provide:

  • Closed captions for all video lessons.
  • Transcripts for audio content.
  • Descriptive text for visual demonstrations.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some areas are still being improved:

  • Some third-party embedded content may not fully meet accessibility standards.
  • Older course content may be missing captions (we're actively working with instructors to add them).
  • Complex interactive exercises may have limited screen reader support.

Feedback & Assistance

We welcome your feedback on accessibility. If you encounter barriers or have suggestions:

  • Email: accessibility@wiser.com
  • Use our Contact form

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 2 business days and resolve issues promptly.
