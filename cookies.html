Cookie Policy

How and why we use cookies and similar technologies.

Last updated: February 1, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you interact with our platform.

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie TypePurposeDuration
EssentialAuthentication, security, basic functionalitySession / 30 days
PreferencesTheme settings, language, layout preferences1 year
AnalyticsUsage patterns, page views, feature adoption2 years
MarketingAd relevance, campaign effectiveness tracking90 days

3. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function. They enable core features like logging in, accessing course content, and processing payments. You cannot disable these.

4. Preference Cookies

These remember your settings, like dark mode preference (wiser_theme) and your user session. They improve your experience but are not strictly necessary.

5. Analytics Cookies

We use analytics to understand how learners use Wiser, which courses are popular, and where we can improve. Data is aggregated and anonymized wherever possible.

6. Marketing Cookies

These help us show relevant promotions and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. You can opt out of these at any time.

7. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Note: Blocking essential cookies may prevent you from using parts of the platform.

8. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage to save your theme preference and authentication state. This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers with every request.

9. Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Check this page periodically for the latest information.

10. Contact

Questions about our use of cookies? Email us at privacy@wiser.com.
